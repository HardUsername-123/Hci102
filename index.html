This place was known as the Whispering Woods, whispered to be cursed by those who lived nearby. Legend had it that the trees themselves whispered secrets of the past and the future to those who dared to listen, driving them to madness or worse. On a moonless night, Sarah, a young woman with a penchant for adventureto lean in closer.

Suddenly, she heard it – a faint whisper, barely audible above the rustling leaves. Sarah froze, her heart pounding in her chest. She strained to listen, her breath catching in her throat. The whisper grew louder, more insistent, filling her mind with eerie visions and unsettling thoughts. Desperate to escape the haunting whispers, Sarah ran blindly through the forest, the trees closing in around her like a suffocating embrace. But no matter how fast she ran, the whispers followed, their voices growing louder and more menacing with each passing moment.
